AboutMe

Software Developer!

Hello! I’m Zarar Imtiaz, a third-year Computer Engineering student at Toronto Metropolitan University with a strong passion for building innovative and impactful tech solutions. Over the past few years, I’ve worked on a wide range of projects using platforms like Arduino and languages such as C++, Java, and Python. Some of my favorite experiences include developing a firefighter robot, recreating Super Mario Bros, and building responsive web applications. These projects have helped me grow as both a software and hardware developer, sharpening my skills in problem-solving, design, and user experience. Currently, I’m 20 years old and working on an AI-powered chatbot project, where I’m diving deeper into machine learning and natural language processing. I’m also continuously learning new technologies, especially in web development and AI, and I enjoy challenging myself with projects that push me outside my comfort zone. I’m passionate about using technology to solve real-world problems and excited for the opportunities ahead in the world of innovation and engineering.
